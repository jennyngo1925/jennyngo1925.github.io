Projects


Cinema E-Booking Website

MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js (TypeScript), tRPC, Next.js, HTML, CSS

This website allows you to book movie tickets and reserve seats online. You can watch trailers, view showing times, and search for movies. The video showcases the functionality and privileges of normal users and system administrators. Users can purchase tickets while administrators can add movies, edit movie information and showtimes, add promotions, and suspend users. We used password salting and hashing to ensure the security of our users.

A recipe blog with some of my favorite dessert recipes for big sweet tooths that allows you to log in and leave reviews!


Shopping List

Android Studio (Java), Firebase

An organization app that helps roommates keep track of and settle expenses for household items. Roommates can register an account to add items to the shopping list. Then, roommates can add them to purchases once they've purchased them. All roommates will be able to see the updated lists and settle the cost per roommate. Once costs are settled, the purchases are removed.


State Capitals Quiz

Android Studio (Java), SQLite

A trivia app that presents you with 6 states and asks you to choose the correct capital. In the end, it will present your score and you can also see your past quiz results. It also saves your progress if you leave the app and come back to it.
