About Me

Hi! My name is Jenny Ngo and I am a senior at the University of Georgia studying computer science. During both my academic journey and professional experience, I have honed my abilities in various programming languages, including Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, C#, and C++. At Goldman Sachs, I had the opportunity to work on an automated task scheduler using Node.js, TypeScript, MongoDB, and Jest, eliminating manual tasks to 0!! These experiences have enabled me to contribute effectively to both individual and team-based projects, as well as to deliver high-quality software solutions. I'm eager to apply my technical expertise and my drive for continuous learning to contribute to make a real impact.
